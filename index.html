About

I am a PhD researcher in Energetics at Politecnico di Torino, working on hydrogen-based internal combustion engines for zero-emission mobility. My research combines 3D CFD analysis of in-cylinder processes with 0D/1D modelling and design of experiments to develop and validate innovative, fuel-flexible combustion models. I also collaborate with industrial and academic partners and serve as a teaching assistant in the Thermal Machines course.

Research interests

Hydrogen ICEs

Combustion modelling and optimization of H₂ internal combustion engines for zero-emission transport

3D CFD

In-cylinder flow, mixture formation and combustion simulation using CONVERGE

0D/1D modelling

Engine cycle simulation and design of experiments with GT-Power and Gasdyn

Alternative fuels

Fuel-flexible approaches for CNG, hydrogen and future low-carbon powertrains
