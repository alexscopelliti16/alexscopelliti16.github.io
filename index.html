Alex Scopelliti PhD Researcher · Energetics · Politecnico di Torino LinkedIn ResearchGate Email Politecnico di Torino Torino, Italy alex.scopelliti@polito.it About I am a PhD researcher in Energetics at Politecnico di Torino, working on hydrogen-based internal combustion engines for zero-emission mobility. My research combines 3D CFD analysis of in-cylinder processes with 0D/1D modelling and design of experiments to develop and validate innovative, fuel-flexible combustion models. I also collaborate with industrial and academic partners and serve as a teaching assistant in the Thermal Machines course.
